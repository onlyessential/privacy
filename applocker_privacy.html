Privacy Policy for AppLock

Developer: OnlyZero  •  Contact: onlyapp.contact@gmail.com
Last updated: October 25, 2025

No data collection Runs on-device No analytics/ads SDKs Permissions are optional & revocable

1) Overview

AppLock helps you secure selected apps on your Android device. All core functionality runs locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal data with external servers or third parties.

2) Permissions We Use & Why

2.1 Accessibility Service (Optional)

Purpose: Detect when a protected app is opened and display the lock screen overlay to prevent unauthorized access.

  • We do not read or record on-screen text, chat content, passwords, or typed input.
  • We do not capture screenshots, screen content, audio, or camera input.
  • All accessibility signals are processed locally and are not transmitted.

Control: Enable/disable anytime in your device’s Accessibility settings.

2.2 Device Administrator (Optional; Anti-Uninstall)

Purpose: Prevent uninstallation when Anti-Uninstall Protection is enabled.

  • We do not wipe your device, reset passwords, or change system configurations.

Control: Revoke Device Admin anytime in system settings.

2.3 Usage Access (Optional; Alternative Detection Method)

Purpose: Determine which app is currently in the foreground so AppLock knows when to show the lock screen.

  • No usage data leaves your device.
  • We do not store, sell, or share app usage information.

Control: Grant or revoke in Usage Access settings.

2.4 Overlay / “Display Over Other Apps”

Purpose: Show the AppLock lock screen on top of protected apps.

  • The overlay is purely visual and does not capture the underlying screen or content.

Control: Toggle in system settings.

2.5 Notifications (Optional)

Purpose: Provide reminders or status notifications related to protection features.

  • We do not read, store, or analyze the content of your notifications.

Control: Manage via system notification settings.

3) Data Collection, Storage, and Sharing

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (AppLock does not operate servers).
  • App configuration (e.g., protected apps, preferences) is stored only on your device.
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs are used.
  • No information is sold or shared.

4) Third-Party Services

AppLock does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5) Your Choices & Controls

  • All permissions are optional (some features may require them).
  • You can revoke any permission at any time in device settings.
  • You can uninstall AppLock at any time (ensure Anti-Uninstall is disabled first).

6) Children’s Privacy

We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7) Data Security

AppLock relies on Android’s sandboxing and permission model. Because AppLock operates locally and does not transmit your data, exposure risk is minimized.

8) Policy Changes

We may update this Policy to reflect improvements or legal changes. Updates will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
