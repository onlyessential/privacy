Privacy Policy

AppLocker

Last updated: October 2025

1. Overview

AppLocker (“the App”, “we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled. We do not collect, store, or share personal data of any kind.

2. Information We Collect

AppLocker does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. The App may use temporary system data to detect open apps and enforce lock protection — all processed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Permissions Used

⚠️ AccessibilityService Usage:

AppLocker uses the AccessibilityService API solely to detect when a locked app is opened and display the lock screen.

This service is optional and activated only after your explicit consent via a clear two-button dialog.

We never log, record, or share any on-screen content or user interactions.

The Accessibility permission is not used for advertising, analytics, or any kind of data collection.
  • Usage Access Permission: Checks which app is active to enforce protection. Data stays on your device.
  • Overlay Permission: Allows showing the secure lock overlay over other apps.

4. Data Sharing

AppLocker does not share, sell, or transmit any user information to any third party.

5. Security

The App operates entirely on your device. We use Android’s built-in security layers to help protect your data, but users are encouraged to keep their device software updated and secured.

6. Children’s Privacy

AppLocker is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Updated versions will appear in-app and on our official website.
